Frequently Asked Questions


USDCash is fully collateralized by US dollar deposits. This means that for every 1 token of USDCash issued, there is $1 US Dollar on deposit in our escrow account.

Our escrow structure is deliberately designed so that only USDCash token holders can access the funds. Funds are be used for any reason like transaction in feature stores and for buying and selling multiple assets.

  • Funds held in US-domiciled escrow account.
  • Regular third-party audits of deposits on file.
  • Contractual controls ensure funds available for redemption.

USDCash is able to maintain a stable price of exactly $1.00 because it can always be created for $1.00, or redeemed for $1.00. Our verified, transparent, and audited US-domiciled escrow accounts give holders full confidence in their ability to redeem USDCash for $1.00, ensuring a consistent peg.
Stellar is the ideal blockchain for payments and third-party assets. Transactions on Stellar are orders of magnitude faster and cheaper than on Ethereum or other blockchains; transactions settle in 4-6 seconds and cost well under 1 cent. Additionally, Stellar's native support for third-party assets makes it an ideal choice. There is no need for a custom ERC-20 smart contract which could have bugs or even security vulnerabilities. Because we're built on Stellar as a native third-party asset, USDCash is backed by the full power of the Stellar blockchain.
